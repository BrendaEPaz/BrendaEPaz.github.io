Packages


The sessions include a variety of shots – full family group, individuals, and different combinations of loved ones. If it's your first time in front of the camera and you feel unsure about posing, don't worry - I will guide you through the entire process, and together we'll achieve beautiful results. Once the shutter has closed, the magic continues as I edit each photograph, enhancing colors and compositions to cinematically showcase your family's love and spirit against Los Cabos' striking backdrops.

On the delivery day, you'll be treated to a personal viewing of every final edited image, allowing you to select the number prescribed in your chosen package and you'll receive all selected photos in high-resolution digital format. However, if certain images speak to you more than expected, you have the flexibility to upgrade and expand your collection right then and there. Let me immortalize your family's special moments in the stunning backdrop of Los Cabos.
